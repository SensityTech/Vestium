Everything in one window

A full clothing pipeline, built for the way creators actually work.

Import, preview, fix, and ship — with undo/redo on everything and projects you can reopen months later.

3D preview without GTA V

See your garment on a full freemode ped — skinned, animated with an idle, and tinted with the real palette shader. No game launch, no guesswork.

skinningidle animpalette tint

Reopen any resource

Drop in a compiled pack — yours or a DurtyClothTool one — and Vestium rebuilds an editable project from the YMT: positions, heels, flags, texture letters and all.

round-tripDCT-compatible

Bundles & gen9 locking

Manage many packs in one project. Console-only dummy slots are detected and locked, so a garment can never land on a dead slot and break in-game.

multi-packgen9 aware

Optimize with confidence

A real optimization toolbox: DDS / mipmap / power-of-two texture optimizer, genuine Blender decimation written back into the YDD, missing-LOD generation, and a duplicate analyzer that catches retextured clones across your whole pack. Run any of it in bulk, in parallel, with real progress.

texture optimizerBlender decimateLOD genduplicate analysis

Git-friendly collaboration

Projects are plain folders. Built-in Git & LFS with an incremental, resumable push means a 39 GB pack survives a dropped connection — and your team stays in sync.

Git + LFSresumable

Build for FiveM

Addon or R* DLC replacement. The YMT, shop meta and creature meta are generated clean-room and verified byte-identical to real released packs.

addonDLC replacebyte-identical
